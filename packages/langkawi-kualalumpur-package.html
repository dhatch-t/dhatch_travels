Langkawi and Kuala Lumpur

4 Star Package for 6 Days & 5 Nights

Details

  • 2 Nights Langkawi , 3 Nights Kuala-Lumpur
  • All 5 nights accommodation
  • Daily breakfast included
  • Private Airport Transfer & Intercity Transfer
  • SIC Langkawi Island Hopping with private Return Transfer
  • Private Half Day KL City Tour
  • Private Full Day Genting Tour with Cable Car

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive Langkawi🢒

  • Transfer from Langkawi Airport to Langkawi Hotels upon arrival

    • Day 2: Half day Langkawi Island Hoping 🢒

    • Pickup Time: 8:45 AM
    • Breakfast at the hotel
    • Experience Half Day Island Hopping in Langkawi with Entrance to Tasik Dayang Bunting
    • Trip on a speedboat through the southern waters of Langkawi, exploring the many islands
    • Visit Pregnant Maiden Island for a refreshing dip in a freshwater lake
    • Learn about the local belief that the mountain on this island resembles a pregnant lady
    • Continue to Beras Basah Island, known for its white sandy beach
    • Opportunity for water activities such as parasailing and banana boat rides (at your own expense)
    • Proceed to Singa Besar Island for eagle feeding and close-up views of eagle species
    • Transfer back to the hotel

    Day 3: Arrive Kuala Lumpur 🢒

    • Breakfast at the hotel and check out from Langkawi
    • Transfer from Langkawi Hotel to Langkawi Airport
    • Arrival at KLIA Airport (KLIA 1/KLIA 2) (Internal flight to be booked separately or by yourslef)

    Day 4: Kuala lumpur city tour🢒

    • Breakfast at the hotel.
    • Check-out from Sunway Hotel.
    • Proceed to experience Half Day Kuala Lumpur City Tour.
    • Experience the unique blend of modern, historic, secular, and religious sights found in Kuala Lumpur during this half-day sightseeing tour.
    • Places of Visits: King's Palace | National Monument | Independence Square | Petronas Twin Towers photostop.
    • Transfer to the hotel and check-in.

    Day 5: Full day Genting Highland tour 🢒

    • Pickup Time: 10:00 AM
    • Breakfast at the hotel
    • Continue journey to Genting Highland with enroute Batu Caves for photostop
    • 2 Ways Skyway Return Transfer; cable car rides will be provided
    • Any entrance tickets for the Themepark - guests will need to purchase on their own at the counter
    • Short photostop at Batu Caves for about 20 minutes
    • The magnificent Batu Caves are one of the top tourist attractions in Malaysia with 272 colorful steps leading to Hindu temples
    • Begin your journey to Genting Highlands with a scenic drive along the mountains
    • Driver will stop at the cable car stop points (Awana Skyway/Genting Skyway) for cable car rides
    • Ride the fastest mono cable car in the world and enjoy panoramic views of the tropical rainforest
    • Visit Malaysia's only international casino resort, Casino de Genting
    • Explore over 3,000 machines and 426 tables, Asia's version of Las Vegas
    • Enjoy time shopping at Genting Highland premium outlets with many branded shops
    • Transfer back to the hotel

    Day 6: Departure 🢒

    • Breakfast at the hotel
    • Transfer from Kuala Lumpur Hotels to KLIA Airport (KLIA 1/KLIA 2)
