Dubai and Abudhabi 5D/4N

5 Days 4 Nights

Details

  • Visit iconic landmarks like the Burj Khalifa, Dubai Museum, and Jumeirah Beach.
  • Enjoy a desert safari with dune bashing and an Arab dance show.
  • Explore Abu Dhabi’s mosque and Ferrari World.
  • Shop for gold at Dubai’s Gold Souk and Dubai Mall.
  • Dine on a deluxe cruise and in the desert under the stars.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai 🢒

  • Departure from Chennai International Airport; arrival in Dubai.
  • Completion of immigration and customs formalities.
  • Hotel check-in.
  • Visit deluxe cruise in Dubai Creek.
  • Evening city tour of Dubai and return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2: Dubai Exploration 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit Dubai Museum, Jumeirah Beach, and Jumeirah Farm to explore key city locations.
  • Afternoon desert safari with thrilling dune bashing rides.
  • Evening Arab dance show and dinner in the desert.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Abu Dhabi Tour🢒

  • After breakfast, take a morning trip to Abu Dhabi.
  • Visit the largest mosque in Asia and explore the Ferrari World exterior.
  • City tour of Abu Dhabi.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Iconic Dubai 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world.
  • Enjoy the high-speed elevator ride and panoramic views from the top of the tower.
  • Explore the Dubai Shopping Mall.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Gold Souk and Departure 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the famous Gold Souk in Dubai to explore exquisite gold and jewelry.
  • Shop for rare and pure gold jewelry in the tax-free market.
  • After shopping, depart for Chennai/Trichy with fond memories.
