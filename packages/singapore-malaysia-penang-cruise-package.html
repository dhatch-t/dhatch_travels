Singapore Malaysia Penang Cruise

6 Nights 7 Days

Details

    • Explore the cultural and natural beauty of Penang, Kuala Lumpur, and Singapore.
    • Visit iconic attractions like Batu Caves, KL Tower, Sentosa Island, and Jurong Bird Park.
    • Enjoy luxurious accommodations including a night aboard the Star Cruise.
    • Travel in comfort with Indian meals, Tamil-speaking guides, and A/C luxury coaches.

    Itinerary

    Day 1: Arrival in Penang🢒

    • After breakfast, explore the Penang Sea Bridge.
    • Visit Water Hill, Murugan Temple, Sleeping Buddha, and the Thousand Buddhas Temple.
    • Spend the night aboard the luxurious Star Cruise.

    Day 2: Penang to Kuala Lumpur🢒

    • After breakfast, visit scenic spots in Penang.
    • Take a bus to Kuala Lumpur.
    • Explore Putrajaya, including the palace and cityscape.

    Day 3: Kuala Lumpur City Tour🢒

    • After breakfast, visit the National Monument and National Memorial.
    • See the Twin Towers and the King’s Palace from the outside.
    • Ascend KL Tower for panoramic views.
    • Overnight stay in Kuala Lumpur.

    Day 4: Batu Caves and Genting Highlands🢒

    • After breakfast, visit Batu Caves.
    • Proceed via cable car to Genting Highlands for indoor theme park activities and relaxation.
    • Overnight stay in Genting.

    Day 5: Kuala Lumpur to Singapore🢒

    • After breakfast, take a bus to Singapore.
    • Check in at the hotel and enjoy a city tour or shopping at your convenience.
    • Overnight stay in Singapore.

    Day 6: Sentosa Island and City Tour🢒

    • After breakfast, enjoy a city tour of Singapore.
    • Post-lunch, take a cable car to Sentosa Island.
    • Visit attractions such as Image of Singapore and Wings of Time.
    • Overnight stay in Singapore.

    Day 7: Jurong Bird Park and Departure🢒

    • After breakfast, visit Jurong Bird Park.
    • Post-lunch, enjoy shopping or exploring bazaars.
    • Head to the airport.
    • Return to Trichy or Chennai with beautiful memories.
