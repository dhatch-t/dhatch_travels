Egypt 5N/6D

5 Nights 6 Days

Details

  • Explore the Great Pyramid of Giza, Papyrus Museum, and Khan el-Khalili Jewelry Factory in Cairo.
  • Visit the National Museum, Roman Theatre, and Papyrus Pillar in Alexandria.
  • Discover the iconic Alexandria Library and historic palaces.
  • Enjoy a panoramic view of Cairo and dine on a Nile cruise.
  • Shop for local crafts and souvenirs in Cairo and Alexandria markets.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Cairo 🢒

  • Departure from Chennai International Airport; arrival in Cairo.
  • Evening at leisure in Cairo for a relaxed start.
  • Overnight stay in Cairo.

Day 2: Cairo Exploration 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the Great Pyramid of Giza, the Papyrus Museum, and the Khan el-Khalili Jewelry Factory.
  • Afternoon at leisure to explore Cairo or enjoy local shopping.
  • Overnight stay in Cairo.

Day 3: Alexandria Tour 🢒

  • After breakfast, depart for Alexandria.
  • Visit the National Museum, Roman Theatre, Papyrus Pillar, and other major attractions.
  • Overnight stay in Alexandria.

Day 4: Alexandria Iconic Sites 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the Palace and the world-famous Alexandria Library.
  • Afternoon at leisure to explore Alexandria further.
  • Overnight stay in Alexandria.

Day 5: Return to Cairo 🢒

  • After breakfast, depart for Cairo.
  • Enjoy a panoramic view of the city.
  • Evening dinner on a Nile cruise, followed by an overnight stay in Cairo.

Day 6: Departure from Cairo 🢒

  • After breakfast, morning departure from Cairo, and arrive in Chennai with cherished memories and divine blessings.
