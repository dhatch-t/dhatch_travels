Sri Lanka 7N/8D

7 Nights 8 Days

Details

  • Visit sacred temples like Mannawari, Nallur Kandaswamy, and Kathirkamam Murugan Temple.
  • Explore historic sites such as the Dutch Fort in Jaffna and Galle Fort.
  • Experience Nuwara Eliya's beauty with visits to Ramboda Hanuman Temple and Seetha Eliya.
  • Visit the UNESCO-listed Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic in Kandy.
  • Enjoy a city tour of Colombo, including Colombo Beach, Parliament, and Independence Square.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo 🢒

  • Depart from Chennai/Madurai airport with blessings, arrive in Colombo, complete customs formalities, and exit the airport.
  • After lunch, proceed to Anuradhapura for an overnight stay.
  • On the way, visit Mannawari, where Lord Shiva is said to have created a Lingam from sand while searching for Sita. Visit the Mannawari temple for darshan.
  • Check-in at the hotel for the night.

Day 2:Anuradhapura to Jaffna 🢒

  • After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and travel from Anuradhapura via Mannar to Triketeswaram (Trikoneswaram) for a visit to the Shiva temple.
  • Continue on to Jaffna for an overnight stay.
  • After lunch, visit Jaffna University and the Dutch Fort Library.
  • After dinner, check-in at the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Nainatheevu Visit 🢒

  • After breakfast, head to Nainatheevu (Nainativu) and visit the Nagapooshani Amman Temple.
  • After dinner, check-in at the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Nallur Kandaswamy Temple and Mullivaikkal 🢒

  • After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and visit the Nallur Kandaswamy Temple (a renowned Murugan temple).
  • Visit Mullivaikkal, the site of the final battle between the Sri Lankan military and the Tamil Tigers.
  • Continue to Trincomalee and visit the Trincomalee Temple.
  • Visit Trincomalee (Trikoneswaram) Shiva Temple for darshan and proceed to Dambulla for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Dambulla to Kandy 🢒

  • After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and visit the Dambulla Cave Temple (Golden Temple).
  • Explore the Botanical Gardens in Peradeniya.
  • Continue to Kandy for an overnight stay.

Day 6: Kandy and Nuwara Eliya 🢒

  • After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa) in Kandy.
  • Then, head to Nuwara Eliya, visiting the Ramboda Hanuman Temple, Nuwara Eliya Lake, and Seetha Eliya (the place where Sita is said to have been hidden).
  • Continue to Kathirkamam for an overnight stay.

Day 7: Kathirkamam and Colombo🢒

  • Early morning, visit the Kathirkamam Murugan Temple to offer prayers and darshan to Lord Murugan, Valli, and Deivanai.
  • After breakfast, check-out and proceed via Kele to the Dutch Fort Museum in Galle.
  • Travel to Colombo, visit the Bazaar, and check-in at the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 8: Colombo City Tour and Departure 🢒

  • After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and enjoy a sightseeing tour of Colombo: visit Colombo Beach, the Parliament, and Independence Square.
  • Depart for the airport to return to Chennai/Madurai, concluding your trip with cherished memories.
