Burj Khalifa & Abu Dhabi

6 Days 5 Nights

Details

  • 5-night accommodation in a 4-star hotel with breakfast included
  • Return transfers from Dubai Airport
  • Private arrival transfer to Marina (Deluxe - Alishba or similar)
  • Private city tour, including Burj Khalifa and Aquarium
  • Private tour of View at The Palm and a premium desert safari
  • Shared tour to Abu Dhabi with Ferrari experience
  • All applicable hotel taxes (GST extra)

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai 🢒

  • Arrive at Dubai International Airport, complete immigration formalities, and collect luggage.
  • Proceed to the arrival hall, greeted by a local representative for your transfer to the hotel.
  • Check-in and relax at your hotel.
  • Overnight stay in Dubai.

Day 2: Dubai Half-Day City Tour and Dhow Cruise with Dinner 🢒

  • Enjoy breakfast at the hotel.
  • Embark on a half-day city tour of Dubai, visiting Bur Dubai Creek and the Spice Market.
  • Stop for photos at Burj Al Arab, Palm Island, and Atlantis the Palm Hotel.
  • Visit Jumeirah Mosque (appropriate dress required).
  • In the evening, enjoy a Dhow Cruise with dinner, scenic views, and entertainment.
  • Overnight stay in Dubai.

Day 3: Desert Safari 🢒

  • Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
  • Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Morning free for leisure activities.
  • In the afternoon, enjoy a desert safari with dune-bashing, camel rides, henna, and shisha.
  • Evening barbecue dinner with belly dance and music under the Arabian sky.
  • Overnight stay in Dubai.

Day 4: Visit Burj Khalifa 🢒

  • Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Head to Burj Khalifa, visiting the observation deck on the 124th floor.
  • Enjoy panoramic views of Dubai Marina, Burj Al Arab, and more.
  • Rest of the day free for leisure exploration.
  • Overnight stay in Dubai.

Day 5: Abu Dhabi City Tour with Ferrari World Bronze Ticket 🢒

  • After breakfast, day tour of Abu Dhabi including Sheikh Zayed Mosque and Emirates Palace.
  • Visit Abu Dhabi Museum.
  • Afternoon visit to Ferrari World Theme Park with thrilling rides.
  • Return to Dubai in the evening for overnight stay.

Day 6: Departure 🢒

  • After breakfast, check out and transfer to the airport for departure.
