Almaty Expedition 5D/4N

5 Days / 4 Nights

Details

    • Explore the scenic beauty and cultural landmarks of Almaty.
    • Visit iconic attractions such as the Presidential Palace, Republic Place, and Tien-Shan Mountains.
    • Enjoy shopping at the Green Bazaar and outdoor activities like skating.
    • Experience authentic Indian cuisine and stay in luxurious star-rated hotels.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Almaty🢒

  • Depart from Delhi and arrive in Almaty.
  • Check in to your hotel and relax after the journey.
  • Optional evening entertainment program or dinner.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Almaty.

Day 2: City Tour🢒

  • After breakfast, explore Central City Park (28 Panfilov Heroes).
  • Visit the Russian Orthodox Cathedral Church.
  • Walk through the Presidential Alley.
  • See the Eternal Flame and the Museum of Folk Musical Instruments.
  • Shop at the Green Bazaar.
  • Visit Republic Place.
  • Relax at the hotel.
  • Optional evening entertainment or dinner.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3: Scenic Tour and Activities🢒

  • After breakfast, visit Dostyk Avenue and Downtown Almaty.
  • Explore a high-end apple orchard.
  • Enjoy activities such as skating at the natural park and skating area or other outdoor games.
  • Visit Antimud Dam and Shymbulak Mountains via cable car.
  • Return to the hotel, have dinner, and rest.

Day 4: Mountain Excursion🢒

  • Visit the Shymbulak range and Tien-Shan Mountains.
  • Enjoy lunch followed by rest.
  • Spend a relaxing evening with dinner.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Departure🢒

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel.
  • Proceed to Delhi Airport for your return journey with cherished memories.
  • Continue onward to Chennai.
