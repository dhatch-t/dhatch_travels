Shimla, Manali, Chandigarh 3 Star Package

7 Days 6 Nights

Details

  • Embark on a scenic road journey from New Delhi/Chandigarh to Shimla, enjoying picturesque hills and valleys along the way. Upon arrival, check in at your hotel and relax or explore the local area.
  • Visit Kufri, a hill station offering panoramic views, and explore Shimla's Zoological Park and Mall Road, known for its colonial charm and vibrant atmosphere.
  • Travel to Manali, enjoying the scenic drive through valleys and along the Beas River. Check in at your hotel and unwind after the journey.
  • Explore the Hadimba Temple, nestled in cedar forests, and the Vashisht Temple with its natural hot water springs, along with the Club House for indoor games and activities.
  • Take a trip to Rohtang Pass or Snow Point for adventure activities such as skiing or paragliding, and visit Solang Valley for more outdoor fun and breathtaking views.
  • Drive to Chandigarh, explore the local markets or Sukhna Lake, and enjoy a relaxing evening before checking out and proceeding to your onward destination.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Shimla🢒

  • After arrival at New Delhi/Chandigarh airport/railway station, transfer by road to Shimla.
  • Enjoy the scenic drive through the hills and valleys as you approach Shimla.
  • Check-in at the hotel in Shimla and spend the evening at leisure exploring the local area or relaxing at the hotel.
  • Enjoy a delicious dinner and a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Shimla Sightseeing🢒

  • After breakfast, visit Kufri, situated at an altitude of 2500 meters. Enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding hills.
  • In the afternoon, visit the Zoological Park, home to a variety of Himalayan wildlife, and then head to Mall Road for some shopping.
  • Explore the local cafes and take in the colonial architecture of the area.
  • In the evening, enjoy a hearty dinner at the hotel and relax with an overnight stay.

Day 3: Shimla to Manali (257kms/7hrs)🢒

  • After breakfast, depart for Manali, a picturesque hill station at an altitude of 1929 meters.
  • Enjoy a scenic drive through the mountains and along the Beas River, offering picturesque views of the valleys and landscapes.
  • On arrival, check-in at the hotel and unwind from the journey.
  • Spend the evening at leisure, exploring the Manali market or simply relaxing at the hotel.
  • Enjoy dinner at the hotel and retire for the night.

Day 4: Manali Local Sightseeing🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the 450-year-old Hadimba Temple, located amidst the cedar forests, and enjoy the tranquil surroundings.
  • Next, visit the Vashisht Temple, known for its natural hot water springs, and Club House for some indoor games and fun activities.
  • In the evening, enjoy a leisurely stroll around the Old Manali area, filled with cafes and quaint shops.
  • Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 5: Excursion to Rohtang Pass and Solang Valley🢒

  • After breakfast, take a half-day excursion to Rohtang Pass/Snow Point, where you can experience the snow-capped mountains and indulge in snow activities.
  • If the pass is closed, head to Solang Valley, known for adventure sports like paragliding, zorbing, and skiing (subject to snow conditions).
  • On the way back, explore the picturesque Solang Valley and enjoy breathtaking views of the snow-clad peaks.
  • Return to the hotel, enjoy a hot dinner, and relax overnight.

Day 6: Manali to Chandigarh (320Kms/8Hrs)🢒

  • After breakfast, drive to Chandigarh, passing through beautiful landscapes and small villages.
  • On arrival, check-in at the hotel and relax after the long journey.
  • Spend the evening at leisure, exploring the local markets, or visit Sukhna Lake for a peaceful boat ride.
  • Enjoy dinner at the hotel and a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 7: Departure from Chandigarh🢒

  • After breakfast, you will be driven to your chosen city for departure. Optionally, visit the Rock Garden or Rose Garden in Chandigarh before leaving.
  • Reflect on the beautiful memories of your trip as you make your way to the airport or railway station for your onward journey.
