China 9D/8N

9 Days 8 Nights

Details

  • Visit Beijing’s Tiananmen Square, Forbidden City, Great Wall, Summer Palace, and Temple of Heaven.
  • Explore Shanghai’s skyline, Oriental Pearl Tower, Nanjing Road, and Huangpu River boat ride.
  • Experience Hong Kong’s Victoria Park, Madame Tussauds, and Disneyland.
  • Discover Macau’s St. Paul’s Church, Fisherman’s Wharf, and Lotus Square.
  • Enjoy bullet train and ferry rides with guided tours throughout the trip.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Beijing 🢒

  • Depart from Chennai/Trichy and arrive in Beijing.
  • Complete immigration & customs procedures and check-in to hotel
  • Enjoy a Kung Fu show and dinner.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Beijing Sightseeing 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit Tiananmen Square, the largest city square in the world.
  • Explore the Forbidden City and its Imperial Palace.
  • Visit the Summer Palace in the afternoon.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Great Wall and Olympic Stadium🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the Great Wall of China, one of the world’s wonders.
  • See the Beijing Olympic Stadium from the outside.
  • Explore the Temple of Heaven.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Bullet Train to Shanghai🢒

  • After breakfast, travel by Bullet Train to Shanghai.
  • Enjoy an evening Acrobatic Show in Shanghai.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Explore Shanghai🢒

  • Visit the Jade Buddha Temple.
  • Explore the Oriental Pearl Tower and Nanjing Road for shopping.
  • Visit the World Expo site from the 2010 Shanghai Expo.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 6: Travel to Hong Kong and Macau 🢒

  • Depart from Shanghai and reach Hong Kong.
  • Take a ferry to Macau.
  • Explore the iconic St. Paul’s Church, Kum Lam Statue, Lotus Square, and Fisherman’s Wharf.
  • Return to the hotel in Hong Kong for an overnight stay.

Day 7: Macau to Hong Kong 🢒

  • Depart from Macau and return to Hong Kong.
  • Visit Victoria Park, Aberdeen Bay, and the Madame Tussauds Wax Museum.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 8: Disneyland Fun 🢒

  • After breakfast, enjoy the magical experience at Hong Kong Disneyland.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 9: Departure 🢒

  • After breakfast, check out of the hotel and head to the airport for your flight back to Chennai/Trichy.
  • Arrive with cherished memories.
