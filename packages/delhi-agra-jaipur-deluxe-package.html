Delhi, Agra and Jaipur Deluxe Package

5 Nights 6 Days

Details

  • Arrive in Delhi and explore its rich blend of history and modernity.
  • Visit Jama Masjid, Raj Ghat, Red Fort (outside), and enjoy a rickshaw ride in Old Delhi.
  • Discover New Delhi’s Qutub Minar, Humayun’s Tomb, India Gate, and Rashtrapati Bhawan.
  • Travel to Agra to marvel at the Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and Itmad-ud-Daulah (Baby Taj).
  • Visit Fatehpur Sikri en route to Jaipur, exploring its grand palaces and mosques.
  • In Jaipur, visit Amer Fort, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar; enjoy shopping in vibrant bazaars.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi 🢒

  • Arrive in Delhi and transfer to your hotel.
  • Relax and take in the city’s blend of ancient and modern attractions.
  • Spend the afternoon exploring the vibrant bazaars or shopping.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing 🢒

  • After breakfast, explore the historical sites of Old Delhi, including Jama Masjid and Raj Ghat.
  • Take a cycle rickshaw ride through the bustling streets of Chandni Chowk.
  • In the afternoon, visit Qutub Minar, Humayun’s Tomb, and drive past India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhawan, and the Parliament.
  • Spend free time shopping in Delhi’s colorful bazaars.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3: Delhi to Agra🢒

  • Drive to Agra and check in to your hotel.
  • Visit the Taj Mahal, a masterpiece of Mughal architecture.
  • Explore Agra Fort, a stunning example of Mughal grandeur, and the Itmad-ud-Daula tomb, also known as the Baby Taj.
  • Shop in Agra’s local markets for handicrafts and souvenirs.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: Agra to Jaipur via Fatehpur Sikri🢒

  • Drive to Jaipur, stopping at Fatehpur Sikri to visit the abandoned Mughal city.
  • Continue to Jaipur, known for its vibrant pink sandstone buildings and rich history.
  • Relax and explore Jaipur’s bustling markets in the afternoon.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Jaipur Sightseeing🢒

  • Visit Amer Fort, a stunning hilltop fort with beautiful views, and take an elephant or jeep ride to the top.
  • Explore the City Palace and Jantar Mantar, an astronomical observatory.
  • Spend the afternoon shopping for jewelry, textiles, and handicrafts in Jaipur’s vibrant bazaars.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6: Departure from Jaipur🢒

  • Check out from the hotel and transfer to Jaipur Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey.
