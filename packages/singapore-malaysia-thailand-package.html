Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand 8N/9D

8 Nights 9 Days

Details

  • Arrival in Bangkok, travel to Pattaya, Alcazar Show, Walking Street.
  • Coral Island tour, Gem Factory, return to Bangkok.
  • Visit Golden Buddha, Reclining Buddha, local market.
  • Travel to Kuala Lumpur, Putrajaya, city tour, Batu Caves, Genting.
  • Travel to Singapore, Merlion Park, Sentosa, shopping, depart for Trichy/Chennai.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bangkok 🢒

  • After breakfast, depart for Pattaya and check-in at the hotel.
  • After a short rest, attend the famous Alcazar Show and visit Walking Street.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2: Coral Island Tour 🢒

  • After breakfast, take a speedboat to Coral Island and enjoy activities like water sports and exploring coral reefs.
  • Visit the Gem Factory in the evening, and then head back to Bangkok and check in to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Bangkok Sightseeing 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the Golden Buddha, the world's largest Reclining Buddha, and a local market.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Travel to Kuala Lumpur 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit Palu and then take a flight to Kuala Lumpur.
  • Visit the new capital of Malaysia, Putrajaya, to see the Palace and the Magati.
  • Check-in to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Kuala Lumpur City Tour 🢒

  • After breakfast, enjoy a city tour of Kuala Lumpur, including the National Mosque, National Monument, the exterior of the Twin Towers, Buddha Temple, the Royal Palace, and KL Tower for a panoramic view of the city.
  • Stay overnight in Kuala Lumpur.

Day 6: Batu Caves & Genting 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the Batu Caves Murugan Temple and then take the cable car to Genting Island.
  • Enjoy the indoor theme park and other attractions at Genting.
  • Stay overnight in Genting Island.

Day 7: Travel to Singapore 🢒

  • After breakfast, take a bus from Kuala Lumpur to Singapore and check in at the hotel.
  • In the evening, visit local markets and shopping areas.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 8: Singapore City Tour🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the Merlion Park, Suntec City, Orchard Road, Mariamman Temple, Chinatown, and the Chinese Temple.
  • After lunch, head to Sentosa Island via cable car and enjoy the 'Wings of Time' laser show.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 9: Shopping & Departure🢒

  • After breakfast, visit local bazaars and enjoy shopping.
  • Depart for Trichy/Chennai with wonderful memories.
