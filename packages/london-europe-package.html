London & Europe 14N/15D

14 Nights 15 Days

Details

  • Explore the landmarks of London, including Buckingham Palace, the London Eye, and Big Ben.
  • Visit Amsterdam’s famous Canal Cruise and the vibrant streets of Belgium.
  • Discover Paris with the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Seine River Cruise.
  • Enjoy the beauty of Switzerland’s Mount Titlis and Germany’s Black Forest.
  • Tour Venice, Pisa, and Rome’s iconic attractions like the Colosseum and St. Peter’s Basilica.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in London 🢒

  • Departure from Chennai; arrival in London.
  • Dinner and hotel check-in.
  • Overnight stay in London.

Day 2: London City Tour 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit Buckingham Palace, London Eye, Tower Bridge, St. Paul’s Cathedral, Big Ben, and Oxford Street.
  • Visit the Tower of London and the Koh-i-Noor Diamond.
  • Overnight stay in London.

Day 3: Madame Tussauds and Shopping 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit Madame Tussauds Wax Museum.
  • Explore Oxford Street and enjoy a luxury cruise on the Stena Line.
  • Overnight stay in London.

Day 4: Amsterdam and Belgium 🢒

  • Arrive at Hook of Holland and explore Mini-Holland, then enjoy the famous Canal Cruise in Amsterdam.
  • In the afternoon, visit Brussels’ Grand Market Square.
  • Overnight stay in Belgium.

Day 5: Paris City Tour 🢒

  • Arrive in Paris; visit Concorde Square, Notre Dame Cathedral, and key landmarks.
  • Overnight stay in Paris.

Day 6: Eiffel Tower and Louvre 🢒

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and enjoy a Seine River Cruise.
  • Overnight stay in Paris.

Day 7: Heidelberg, Germany🢒

  • Arrive in Heidelberg and explore the city.
  • Overnight stay in Heidelberg.

Day 8: Black Forest and Rhine Falls 🢒

  • Explore the Black Forest, Lake Titisee, and Rhine Waterfalls.
  • Arrive in Engelberg for an overnight stay.

Day 9: Mount Titlis and Lucerne 🢒

  • Visit Mount Titlis with cable cars, Glacier Park, and the Ice Grotto.
  • Visit Lucerne’s Chapel Bridge and Lion Monument.
  • Overnight stay in Lucerne.

Day 10: Jungfraujoch, Switzerland🢒

  • Travel to Jungfraujoch, Europe’s highest peak, and explore its famous attractions.
  • Evening in Interlaken.
  • Overnight stay in Interlaken.

Day 11: Innsbruck, Austria 🢒

  • Visit Innsbruck and explore the Golden Roof and Swarovski Crystal Museum.
  • Overnight stay in Innsbruck.

Day 12: Venice, Italy 🢒

  • Arrive in Venice and enjoy a boat ride to St. Mark’s Square, Doge’s Palace, and the Bridge of Sighs.
  • Overnight stay in Venice.

Day 13: Pisa and Rome 🢒

  • Explore Pisa’s Leaning Tower and the Pisa Cathedral.
  • Evening departure to Rome.
  • Overnight stay in Rome.

Day 14: Rome City Tour 🢒

  • Visit St. Peter’s Basilica, the Colosseum, and the Trevi Fountain.
  • Overnight stay in Rome.

Day 15: Departure from Rome 🢒

  • Morning departure for Rome Airport.
  • Return to Chennai with unforgettable memories.
