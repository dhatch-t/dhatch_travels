Gangtok and Darjeeling Package

5 Days 4 Nights

Details

  • Visit Tsomgo Lake, Baba Mandir, and experience breathtaking high-altitude landscapes.
  • Travel comfortably between Gangtok and Darjeeling, enjoying the scenic beauty of the hills.
  • Take a private transfer for a seamless journey between Gangtok and Darjeeling.
  • Witness the mesmerizing sunrise from Tiger Hill and explore key attractions like the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute and Tenzing Rock.
  • Stroll through the Tea Gardens, visit the Japanese Temple, and shop for unique handicrafts at the Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre.

Itinerary

Day 1:Arrival at NJP Rly Station / IXB Airport - Transfer to Gangtok🢒

  • Arrive at New Jalpaiguri Railway Station / Bagdogra International Airport and proceed to Gangtok (5,500 Ft.).
  • Check-in at the hotel upon reaching Gangtok.
  • Take some time to relax and unwind at the hotel.
  • In the evening, explore the local market for souvenirs or enjoy a leisurely stroll through the scenic town.
  • Stay overnight at Gangtok.

Day 2: Excursion to Tsomgo Lake and Baba Mandir 🢒

  • Post breakfast, embark on a scenic drive to Tsomgo Lake (12,400 ft. / 37 Kms. / 02 Hrs. from Gangtok).
  • Admire the stunning natural beauty of the lake and its surroundings.
  • Continue your journey to Baba Mandir (13,200 ft. / 16 Kms. / 01 Hr. from Tsomgo Lake) and explore the temple.
  • Return to Gangtok in the evening and relax at the hotel.
  • Stay overnight at Gangtok.

Day 3: Transfer from Gangtok to Darjeeling🢒

  • Enjoy breakfast before heading to Darjeeling (6,950 Ft.).
  • Check in at your Darjeeling hotel upon arrival.
  • Relax and enjoy the breathtaking views of the hill station.
  • Spend the evening exploring local cafes and shopping for traditional items.
  • Stay overnight at Darjeeling.

Day 4: Full Day Darjeeling Sightseeing with Tiger Hill🢒

  • Set out early (4:00 AM) to visit Tiger Hill for an unforgettable sunrise view of the Kanchenjunga range (subject to permit availability).
  • After breakfast, visit the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, P.N. Zoological Park (closed on Thursdays), Tenzing Rock, and take a ride on the Ropeway.
  • Explore the Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre (closed on Sundays) and discover local handicrafts.
  • Take a scenic drive around the Tea Garden for outer views and visit the peaceful Japanese Temple.
  • Enjoy some free time for shopping or simply relaxing in the evening.
  • Stay overnight at Darjeeling.

Day 5: Departure from Darjeeling to NJP Rly Station / IXB Airport🢒

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and depart for New Jalpaiguri Railway Station / Bagdogra International Airport.
  • If time permits, take a brief visit to Darjeeling Mall Road for some last-minute shopping or sightseeing.
