Thailand Thrills: Bangkok & Pattaya

4 Nights and 5 Days

Details

  • 2 Nights in Pattaya, 2 Nights in Bangkok
  • All accommodation at 3-4 star hotels
  • Daily breakfast included
  • Private airport transfer upon arrival in Bangkok
  • Private intercity transfer from Pattaya to Bangkok
  • Safari World, Marine Park, Coral Island, Nong Nooch Village, Gems Gallery, Reclining Buddha, Floating Market

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bangkok and Pattaya 🢒

  • Afternoon: Arrive at Bangkok airport and proceed to Pattaya.
  • Evening: Check in to the hotel in Pattaya and watch the famous Alcazar Show.
  • Overnight: Stay at the hotel in Pattaya.

Day 2: Coral Island Tour 🢒

  • Morning: After breakfast, take a speedboat to Coral Island. Enjoy water sports activities like parasailing and underwater walking (additional charges may apply).
  • Afternoon: Return to Pattaya and visit Gems Gallery, the largest jewelry store in the world.
  • Evening: Visit the walking street.
  • Overnight: Stay at the hotel in Pattaya.

Day 3: Nong Nooch Village🢒

  • After breakfast, visit Nong Nooch Village
  • Watch the cultural performances, the thai boxing, and a beautiful botanical garden.
  • Dinner and overnight Stay at the hotel

Day 4: Bangkok City Tour - Safari World and Marine Park 🢒

  • After breakfast, depart for Bangkok and visit the Golden Buddha Statue and the World's Longest Reclining Buddha Statue.
  • Enjoy lunch, then explore the city and do some local shopping.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Safari World and Departure 🢒

  • After breakfast, vacate the hotel and visit Safari World and Marine Park.
  • Enjoy live shows featuring dolphins, sea lions, and orangutans.
  • In the evening, depart for the airport for your flight.
