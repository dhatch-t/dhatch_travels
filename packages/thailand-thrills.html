Thailand Thrills: Bangkok & Pattaya

Package Duration: 4 Nights and 5 Days

Details

  • 2 Nights in Pattaya, 2 Nights in Bangkok
  • All accommodation at 3-4 star hotels
  • Daily breakfast included
  • Private airport transfer upon arrival in Bangkok
  • Private intercity transfer from Pattaya to Bangkok
  • Safari World, Marine Park, Coral Island, Nong Nooch Village, Gems Gallery, Reclining Buddha, Floating Market

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bangkok and Pattaya 🢒

  • Afternoon: Arrive at Bangkok airport and proceed to Pattaya.
  • Evening: Check in to the hotel in Pattaya. Enjoy free time to explore the local area.
  • Overnight: Stay at the hotel in Pattaya.

Day 2: Coral Island Tour 🢒

  • Morning: After breakfast, take a speedboat to Coral Island. Enjoy water sports activities like parasailing and underwater walking (additional charges may apply).
  • Afternoon: Return to Pattaya and visit Gems Gallery, the largest jewelry store in the world.
  • Evening: Return to the hotel and relax.
  • Overnight: Stay at the hotel in Pattaya.

Day 3: Nong Nooch Village and Bangkok Transfer 🢒

  • Morning: After breakfast, visit Nong Nooch Village, known for cultural performances, Thai boxing, and a beautiful botanical garden.
  • Afternoon: Check out from the hotel in Pattaya and transfer to Bangkok. Visit the Reclining Buddha Temple en route.
  • Evening: Check in to the hotel in Bangkok.
  • Overnight: Stay at the hotel in Bangkok.

Day 4: Bangkok City Tour - Safari World and Marine Park 🢒

  • Morning: After breakfast, visit Safari World and Marine Park. Enjoy live shows featuring dolphins, sea lions, and orangutans.
  • Afternoon: Continue exploring the Safari Park, where you can see wildlife in their natural habitat.
  • Evening: Return to the hotel and relax.
  • Overnight: Stay at the hotel in Bangkok.

Day 5: Floating Market and Departure 🢒

  • Morning: After breakfast, visit the famous Floating Market and experience traditional Thai shopping from boats.
  • Afternoon: Check out from the hotel and proceed to Bangkok airport for departure.
