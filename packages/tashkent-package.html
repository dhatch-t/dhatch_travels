Tashkent Treasures 4N/5D

4 Nights 5 Days

Details

  • Explore the vibrant city of Tashkent.
  • Visit iconic landmarks like Independence Square, Amir Temur Square, and the TV Tower.
  • Enjoy snow activities in the Chimgam Mountains.
  • Relax by the picturesque Charvak Lake.
  • Experience Indian meals, shopping, and Gala Party Dinners every evening.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Tashkent 🢒

  • After customs clearance, check into the hotel.
  • Rest and proceed for a city tour to Independence Square and Amir Temur Square.
  • End the day with a Gala Party Dinner.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Chimgam Mountains and Charvak Lake 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit Chimgam Mountains for snow activities.
  • Enjoy Charvak Lake.
  • End the day with a Gala Party Dinner.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3: City Tour and Shopping 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the Lal Bahadur Shastri Memorial.
  • Visit the Tashkent TV Tower.
  • Spend time shopping.
  • End the day with a Gala Party Dinner.

Day 4: Leisure and 3D Show🢒

  • After breakfast, watch a 3D Show.
  • Post-lunch, enjoy free time to explore the city at your leisure.
  • End the day with a Gala Party Dinner.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Departure 🢒

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel.
  • Visit Alay Market, Samarkand Darwaja, and Tsum Hall for shopping.
  • Transfer to the airport for your flight to Delhi and onward to Chennai.
