Dalhousie Getaway

5 Nights 6 Days

Details

  • 5-night stay at well-appointed hotels with breakfast included.
  • Private transfers between Amritsar, Dalhousie, and Dharamshala.
  • Guided tours of Amritsar, Dalhousie, Khajjiar, Dharamshala, and McLeod Ganj.
  • Excursion to Wagah Border for the Beating Retreat Ceremony.
  • Visits to landmarks like the Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh, and Tibetan Monastery.
  • All hotel taxes included (GST extra).

Itinerary

Day 1: Wagah Border Experience 🢒

  • Arrive in Amritsar and transfer to your hotel.
  • Take some time to relax after check-in.
  • Visit the Wagah Border to witness the patriotic Beating Retreat Ceremony with military parades and flag-lowering rituals.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2: Explore Amritsar & Journey to Dalhousie 🢒

  • Check out from the hotel and begin an Amritsar city tour.
  • Visit the Golden Temple, admired for its shimmering beauty and serene ambiance.
  • Explore the Durgiana Temple, a renowned religious site with exquisite architecture.
  • Pay homage at Jallianwala Bagh, a historical landmark commemorating the tragic 1919 massacre.
  • Continue your journey to Dalhousie and check in at the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Day Trip to Khajjiar 🢒

  • Depart for a scenic excursion to Khajjiar, often referred to as "India's Mini Switzerland."
  • Pause at Kalatop to admire stunning valley views.
  • Visit the Kalatop Wildlife Sanctuary, home to diverse plant and animal species.
  • In Khajjiar, see the picturesque Khajjiar Lake and visit the peaceful Khajji Nag Temple.
  • Return to your Dalhousie hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Transfer to Dharamshala & Sightseeing 🢒

  • Check out and proceed to Dharamshala.
  • Check in at the hotel and unwind.
  • Visit the Dharamshala Cricket Stadium, set amidst the breathtaking Himalayas.
  • Explore the Dharamshala War Memorial, honoring the sacrifices of Indian soldiers.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Explore McLeod Ganj 🢒

  • Head out for a day trip to McLeod Ganj.
  • Take in the panoramic views of the Dhauladhar Range from Naddi Village.
  • Discover St. John in the Wilderness Church, a serene Gothic-style structure surrounded by greenery.
  • Visit the Bhagsunath Temple and explore the Tibetan Monastery, adorned with vibrant prayer flags and sacred scriptures.
  • Return to your hotel in Dharamshala for an overnight stay.

Day 6: Return to Amritsar & Departure🢒

  • Check out from the hotel and drive back to Amritsar.
  • Conclude your journey with a transfer to the airport for your onward travel.
