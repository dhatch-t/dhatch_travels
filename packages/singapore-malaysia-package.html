Singapore & Malaysia 6D/5N

6 Days 5 Nights

Details

    • Explore Malaysia’s vibrant new capital, Putrajaya, and the iconic Petronas Twin Towers.
    • Visit Genting Highlands with its indoor theme parks and cool mountain atmosphere.
    • Experience Singapore’s Merlion Park, Sentosa Island, and Jurong Birds Park.
    • Enjoy shopping and city tours in both countries, with opportunities to explore local culture and famous landmarks.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Putrajaya, Malaysia 🢒

  • Depart from Chennai; arrive in Malaysia’s capital, Putrajaya.
  • Visit the Putrajaya Palace and Magath Palace.
  • Evening check-in at the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2: Kuala Lumpur City Tour 🢒

  • After breakfast, begin your Kuala Lumpur city tour.
  • Visit the National Monument and the famous Petronas Twin Towers (outside view).
  • Explore the Buddhist Temple, Royal Palace, and KL Tower.
  • Evening: Enjoy a relaxing dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Kuala Lumpur.

Day 3: Batu Caves and Genting Highlands 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the Batu Caves and Murugan Temple.
  • Explore the Watch Factory before heading to Genting Highlands via a cable car ride.
  • Enjoy indoor theme parks and attractions at Genting Highlands.
  • Evening check-in at a luxury hotel in Genting for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Travel to Singapore 🢒

After breakfast, travel by bus from Kuala Lumpur to Singapore. Check-in at the hotel upon arrival. Spend the afternoon shopping or enjoy a Singapore city tour. Evening at leisure or explore the local sights. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Singapore City Tour 🢒

  • After breakfast, explore the highlights of Singapore.
  • Visit Merlion Park, Suntec City, Orchard Road, Mariamman Temple, Chinatown, and Chinese Temple.
  • After lunch, take a cable car ride to Sentosa Island.
  • In the evening, enjoy the Wings of Time laser show at Sentosa.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 6: Jurong Bird Park and Departure 🢒

  • After breakfast, visit the Jurong Birds Park.
  • After lunch, shop at local bazaars.
  • Evening: Depending on your flight, shop for souvenirs or relax before departure.
  • Take a flight back to Chennai/Trichy with unforgettable memories.
