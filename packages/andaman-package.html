Port Blair, Havelock, and Neil Island

5 Nights 6 Days

Details

  • Accommodation as per mentioned itinerary
  • Daily breakfast at the hotel.
  • Airport transfers.
  • Private ferry from Port Blair to Havelock.
  • Private ferry from Havelock to Neil Island.
  • Private ferry from Neil Island to Port Blair. (If itinerary chosen)
  • Local transportation for sightseeing.
  • Services of a local tour Manager.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair 🢒

  • Arrival at the airport, followed by a transfer to the hotel.
  • Check-in at the hotel (standard check-in time).
  • Visit the Cellular Jail.
  • Attend the captivating Light and Sound Show, which narrates the history of the Cellular Jail.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 2: Port Blair to Havelock - Radhanagar Beach 🢒

  • Enjoy breakfast at the hotel in Port Blair.
  • Head to the Port Blair ferry terminal.
  • Take a ferry to Havelock Island.
  • Arrive at Havelock and transfer to the hotel.
  • Check-in and freshen up.
  • Visit Radhanagar Beach, renowned as one of Asia’s most beautiful beaches.
  • Relax on the white sandy shores and in the turquoise waters.
  • Return to the hotel for leisure time.
  • Overnight stay at Havelock.

Day 3: Havelock - Elephant Beach 🢒

  • Breakfast at the hotel in Havelock.
  • Proceed to Elephant Beach.
  • Experience a thrilling speedboat ride to reach Elephant Beach.
  • Enjoy water sports activities (additional charges apply), including snorkeling, jet skiing, and banana boat rides.
  • Return to the hotel for leisure time to relax or explore the area.
  • Overnight stay at Havelock.

Day 4: Havelock Island - Neil Island + Laxmanpur Beach 🢒

  • Breakfast at the hotel in Havelock.
  • Check out from the hotel.
  • Head to the Havelock jetty for a ferry to Neil Island.
  • Arrive at Neil Island and transfer to the hotel.
  • Check in and freshen up.
  • Visit Laxmanpur Beach, celebrated for its tranquil beauty.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay at Neil Island.

Day 5: Neil Island - Port Blair 🢒

  • Breakfast at the hotel in Neil Island.
  • Visit Bharatpur Beach, famous for its coral reefs and vibrant marine life.
  • Explore the Natural Bridge, a unique rock formation on Neil Island.
  • Check out from the hotel and take a ferry back to Port Blair.
  • Transfer to the hotel in Port Blair and check in.
  • Enjoy free time for personal exploration, shopping, or additional activities.
  • Overnight stay at Port Blair.

Day 6: Departure 🢒

  • Final breakfast at the hotel in Port Blair.
  • Check out from the hotel.
  • Transfer to Port Blair airport for your departure.
  • Depart with cherished memories of your Andaman adventure.
