Kedarnath Spiritual Journey

6 Nights 7 Days

Details

  • Overnight transfer from Delhi to Haridwar, visiting the peaceful Guptakashi and the confluence of the Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers.
  • Trek to Kedarnath Temple, experiencing spiritual presence amidst the Himalayas.
  • Continue the pilgrimage to Badrinath Temple, seeking blessings from Lord Vishnu.
  • Visit Rudraprayag and sacred Prayags, where multiple rivers converge in a peaceful Himalayan setting.
  • Return to Delhi, reflecting on the spiritual blessings and tranquility of the journey.

Itinerary

Day 1: Start trip from Delhi🢒

  • Depart from Delhi in the evening, heading to Haridwar in a Swift Dzire or Innova.
  • Enjoy an overnight journey, arriving in Haridwar the next morning.
  • Relax during the journey and take in the scenic views of the surrounding landscape.
  • En route, enjoy comfort stops to stretch your legs and refresh.

Day 2: Haridwar to Guptakashi🢒

  • Continue the journey to Guptakashi, passing through the confluence of the Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers.
  • Upon reaching Guptakashi, check-in at the hotel and rest for the day.
  • Stop by the Sangam of Alaknanda and Bhagirathi to witness the birth of the Ganga river and feel the divine energy.
  • Enjoy the peaceful ambiance of Guptakashi, surrounded by the majestic Himalayan peaks.
  • Take some time to explore the local markets and shop for souvenirs before returning to the hotel.

Day 3: Guptkashi to Kedarnath🢒

  • Drive from Guptakashi to Sonprayag, then board a government vehicle to Gaurikund, the starting point of the Kedarnath trek.
  • After the Kedarnath darshan, experience the spiritual aura of the Kedarnath Temple nestled amidst the mountains.
  • Spend some time meditating or reflecting in the serene surroundings of Kedarnath.
  • After the darshan, return to your hotel in Kedarnath for a peaceful overnight stay and dinner.
  • Engage in spiritual discussions or read scriptures available at the temple premises.

Day 4: Kedarnath to Guptkashi🢒

  • Trek down from Kedarnath to reach Sonprayag and board your vehicle for the return journey to Guptakashi.
  • On the way back, capture beautiful photographs of the rugged terrain and lush green valleys.
  • After reaching Guptakashi, check-in at the hotel and enjoy some leisure time to relax.
  • Take a short walk around the town or visit nearby temples for an evening prayer session.
  • Enjoy dinner at the hotel, reflecting on the spiritual experiences of the day.

Day 5: Guptkashi to Badrinath🢒

  • After breakfast, proceed from Guptakashi to Badrinath for the darshan of Lord Vishnu.
  • Enjoy the scenic drive through the Garhwal region, passing by mountain streams and quaint villages.
  • Upon arrival in Badrinath, check-in at the hotel and relax.
  • Seek the blessings of Lord Vishnu at the temple, known for its divine aura and spiritual significance.
  • Explore the surrounding areas and enjoy the picturesque views of the Alaknanda river.

Day 6: Badrinath to Rudraprayag🢒

  • Start the day with the Badrinath darshan, soaking in the spiritual energy of the temple.
  • Visit Mana village, the last village on the Indian side, and explore its historical significance.
  • On the way to Rudraprayag, stop at the Prayags (confluences) of the Alaknanda river, including Vishnuprayag, Nandaprayag, Karnaprayag, and Rudraprayag.
  • Enjoy the tranquility of these holy confluences, known for their sacred importance.
  • Arrive in Rudraprayag for an overnight stay, where you can relax and reflect on the journey.

Day 7: Rudraprayag to Delhi🢒

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and begin the journey back to Delhi.
  • As you travel, reflect on the spiritual experiences of the past few days.
  • Arrive in Delhi by evening, concluding the pilgrimage tour with your heart full of divine blessings and memories.
